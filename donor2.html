Benefits and Risks

  1. By donating your data, you are making sure your data can be used again and again. this accelerates research into how our genomes shape our health.
  2. by donating your data, you are taking a small risk that someone might be able to re-identify you as a donor or attempt to harm you.
  3. you can withdraw whenever you want, but we can’t delete copies of data that have already been distributed to researchers.
  4. this study is for people who believe that the benefits are bigger than the risks. we’ll do all in our power to protect your identity while accelerating research.
